FAQ

What is PWM?

Pulse Width Modulation (PWM) is a technique to control the average power delivered to a load by rapidly switching between ON and OFF states. The duty cycle determines the percentage of time the signal is HIGH.

How is average voltage calculated?

Average voltage = Vcc × (Duty Cycle / 100). For a 5V signal at 75% duty cycle, the average voltage is 3.75V.

What is duty cycle?

Duty cycle is the percentage of one period in which the signal is HIGH. A 50% duty cycle means the signal is ON for half the period and OFF for the other half.
