FAQ

What is a Boolean matrix?

A Boolean matrix contains only 0s and 1s. Operations use Boolean algebra: AND (conjunction), OR (disjunction). The Boolean product uses AND for multiplication and OR for addition.

What is the Boolean product of two matrices?

Similar to standard matrix multiplication, but uses AND instead of multiply and OR instead of add. Element C[i][j] = OR of (A[i][k] AND B[k][j]) for all k.

Where are Boolean matrices used?

Boolean matrices are used in graph theory (adjacency matrices), relation theory, database queries, and network routing algorithms.
