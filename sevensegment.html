FAQ

What is a 7-segment display?

A 7-segment display uses seven LED segments (a-g) to show decimal digits 0-9. Each segment can be independently turned on or off to form different digit shapes.

How does BCD to 7-segment decoding work?

A BCD-to-7-segment decoder takes a 4-bit BCD input (0-9) and activates the appropriate segments. Each segment has its own Boolean expression based on the 4 input bits.

What are the segment labels?

Segments are labeled a through g: a is the top horizontal, b and c are the right verticals, d is the bottom horizontal, e and f are the left verticals, and g is the middle horizontal.
